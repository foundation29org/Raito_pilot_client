This is how we manage data

You need to read the complete text of the Privacy Policy for legal purposes, but we really don't like this requirement so we made a summary for you.

1. Where is your data

You choose in which cloud provider you store your patient medical data. We help you create a folder in the cloud. We call this folder: POD (Personal Online Datastore).

2. Who has access

You may share the patient medical data anonymously with your Patient Organization or anyone you want.

You can all times deny or accept access to your folder.

3. Which data we use

In order to use RAITO we need to know some user details: name, last name, email and National ID.

4. Who else uses your data

A third party provider will process your National ID for verification and then it will be deleted.
